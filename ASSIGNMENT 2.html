JELLOF RICE

Jellof rice is a western african delicacy of either Gambia or nigerian origin . it is one of the many ways rice can be prepared, if properly cooked it is tasty and delicious and can be eaten by any race or gender and age (exempting neonates). I like jellof rice because it is delicious and tasty to eat and because it is of african origin.

My fovourite food is JELLOF RICE.

The ingredients for its preparation include:

  • Rice
  • Groundnut oil
  • onion
  • tomatoes
  • salt
  • pepper
  • maggi
  • water
  • spices

The steps for preparing this meal are listed below;

  1. pour a given amount of groundnut oil into the pot on fire🔥
  2. Allow to heat for some minute⌚
  3. Add onion, tomatoes and salt😁
  4. stir the content💥
  5. then add peeper, spices and maggi appropriately🔪.
  6. allow to boil
  7. check for if it has soften
  8. Bring meal down and dish.

The prepared jellof rice should colour yellow to red, be really tasty with a nice aroma associated with tomatoe flavour..it can only smell like jellof rice.,then you will be okay that it is jellof.
